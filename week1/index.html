Pride Pastry

Donuts with a touch of grandeur

Ingredients:

  • 2 ¼ tsp active dry yeast
  • 1 ¼ cups warm milk
  • ¼ cup granulated sugar
  • 2 large eggs
  • 5 tbsp unsalted butter
  • 5 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • 4 cups all-purpose flour
  • ¼ tsp salt
  • for frying
  • 2 cups powdered sugar
  • 3 tbsp milk (for glaze)
  • Food coloring and sprinkles (optional)
Pride goes before destruction, a haughty spirit before a fall.

Steps:

  1. Activate Yeast: a large bowl, combine warm milk and yeast. Let it sit for 5-10 minutes until frothy.
  2. Mix Dough: Add sugar, eggs, melted butter, vanilla, flour, and salt to the yeast mixture. Mix until a dough forms.
  3. Knead Dough: Knead the dough on a floured surface for 5-10 minutes until smooth and elastic. Place in a greased bowl, cover, and let rise for 1-2 hours until doubled in size.
  4. Shape Donuts: Roll out the dough to ½-inch thickness and cut out donuts using a donut cutter. Place them on a baking sheet, cover, and let rise for another 30 minutes.
  5. Fry Donuts: Heat oil in a deep fryer or large pot to 350°F (175°C). Fry the donuts in batches for 1-2 minutes per side until golden brown. Drain on paper towels.
  6. Prepare Glaze: Mix powdered sugar and milk in a bowl. Add food coloring if desired. Dip the donuts in the glaze and add sprinkles.
  7. Serve: Let the glaze set for a few minutes before serving.

Greedy Burger

A towering burger that’s hard to resist.

Ingredients:

  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 4 slices cheddar cheese
  • 4 hamburger buns
  • 2 large eggs
  • Lettuce leaves
  • 1 large tomato, sliced
  • 1 lb ground beef
  • 1 red onion, sliced
  • Pickles
  • Ketchup, mustard, and mayonnaise
Greed is a bottomless pit
exhausts the person in an endless effort to satisfy the need without ever reaching satisfaction.

Steps:

  1. Prepare Patties: Divide the ground beef into 4 equal portions and shape into patties. Season with salt and pepper.
  2. Cook Patties: Heat a grill or skillet over medium-high heat.
    Cook the patties for 4-5 minutes per side until browned and cooked to desired doneness.
    Add a slice of cheese on each patty during the last minute of cooking.
  3. Assemble Burgers: Toast the buns on the grill or in the skillet.
    Spread ketchup, mustard, and mayonnaise on the bottom bun.
    Add lettuce, tomato, onion, pickles, and the cooked patty. Top with the other half of the bun.
  4. Serve: Serve immediately with a side of fries or salad.
